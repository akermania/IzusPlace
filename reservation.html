basic information

Located in the center of Jaco Beach, Cabinas Las Orquideas features an outdoor swimming pool and a garden. Free Wi-Fi and free private parking are available to guests. The rooms at the Cabinas Las Orquideas have a fan. Some of them come with TV with cable channels. The property’s restaurant serves breakfast from Sunday to Friday as well as a Mediterranean dinner. Various restaurants and bars are within 5 minutes’ walk from the property. Shopping centers are a 5 minutes’ drive from the Cabinas Las Orquideas. The property can arrange activities such as tours to the National Reserve of Carara and Manuel Antonio National Park. Cabinas Las Orquideas is 5 minutes’ walk from Jaco Beach and 5 km from Hermosa Beach. Carara Nature Reserve is a 30 minutes’ drive from the property and the capital of San Jose can be reached in less than 90 minutes by car. This is our guests' favorite part of Jacó, according to independent reviews.
